Andy Matthews

I am an architect and the founder of Andy Matthews Studio, a values-driven architecture and design studio based in London, with projects in the UK and USA.

I've lived and worked in London since 2005, and it's where I call home. Before starting my own studio in 2021, I worked at several architectural practices. Alongside architecture, I also worked part time as an architectural photographer from 2009 to 2021.

In my spare time I cycle, take photos for pleasure, swim wherever I can, hang out with my partner Emma and our rescue cat Myrtle, obsessively learn random things, and try to enjoy everything London offers. I was diagnosed with ADHD in 2021, and have written about my experience and helped many others since.
